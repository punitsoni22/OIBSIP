Mr. Aman Gupta

The Founder and CEO of boAt

INTRODUCTION

Aman Gupta is an Indian entrepreneur who co-founded and serves as the chief marketing officer of the earwear company boAt. He is currently in the news for appearing as a shark on Sony TV’s reality show Shark Tank India. Aman Gupta was birthed in New Delhi, India on Mar 4, 1982. He gained his early education at the Delhi Public School in RK Puram, Delhi. After finishing his intermediate studies, he enrolled in Shaheed Bhagat Singh College in Delhi, where he earned a Bachelor of Commerce (B.Com) degree.

Following his B.Com, he gained the Chartered Accountant (CA) degree from The Institute of Chartered Accountants of India at the request of his family persons. He worked as an assistant manager for Citibank for about two years after becoming a CA, but he did not enjoy his job there. He wanted to do something huge for himself or something, but he didn’t know where to start. After working at Citibank from 2003 to 2005, he co-founded Advanced Telemedia Private Limited with his father.

He used to sell for big brands through his company, but due to the comp any’s growing business in India, the company decided that he would open his own office there. Aman’s company, which he had founded with his father, was closed as a result. Aman Gupta married and attended Indian Business School at the request of his wife, Priya Dagar. He began working as a Senior Management Consultant at KPMG International Limited in 2011, after completing his MBA.
